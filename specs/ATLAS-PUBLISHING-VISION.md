# Atlas Publishing - Company Vision

*Ideas That Endure*

---

## Mission

Publish essential works on geopolitics, Middle East affairs, and the intersection of technology and power—books that will be read decades from now.

---

## Publishing Model

### Hybrid Publisher

Combines best of traditional and indie:

| Traditional Element | Indie Element |
|---------------------|---------------|
| Editorial curation | Author-friendly terms |
| Professional production | Faster timelines |
| Distribution network | Higher royalties |
| Marketing support | Author ownership |

---

## Imprint Structure

### Atlas Editions (Flagship)
- **Focus:** Geopolitics, international affairs, power analysis
- **Format:** Hardcover → paperback → digital
- **Target:** Educated general reader
- **Price:** $25-35 hardcover

### Maghreb Press
- **Focus:** North Africa, Algeria, diaspora
- **Format:** Paperback, digital
- **Target:** Academic, diaspora, specialists
- **Price:** $18-25 paperback

### Signal Books (Digital-First)
- **Focus:** Timely analysis, extended essays
- **Format:** Ebook, print-on-demand
- **Target:** Newsletter readers, professionals
- **Price:** $9.99-14.99 digital

## Production Process

### AI-Augmented Publishing

| Stage | AI Role | Human Role |
|-------|---------|------------|
| **Acquisition** | Market analysis, comp research | Editorial judgment |
| **Development** | Structural suggestions, fact-checking | Editing, shaping |
| **Copyedit** | First-pass cleanup | Polish, voice preservation |
| **Design** | Cover concepts, layout | Final design decisions |
| **Marketing** | Copy generation, metadata | Strategy, relationships |
| **Distribution** | Analytics, optimization | Channel partnerships |

### Timeline

| Stage | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Acquisition to contract | 2-4 weeks |
| Developmental edit | 4-8 weeks |
| Copyedit/production | 4-6 weeks |
| Marketing prep | 4 weeks |
| **Total** | **6-9 months** |

### Unit Economics (Per Title)
