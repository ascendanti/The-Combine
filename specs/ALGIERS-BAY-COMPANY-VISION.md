# Algiers Bay Company - Company Vision

*Bridge Between Worlds*

---

## Mission

Facilitate premium import/export trade connecting Algeria and North Africa with global markets, specializing in culturally significant goods with authentic provenance.

---

## Core Business

### Import to Algeria/MENA

| Category | Products | Source Markets |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| **Technology** | Electronics, equipment | East Asia, Europe |
| **Luxury Goods** | Fashion, accessories | Europe, USA |
| **Specialty Foods** | Gourmet, organic | Europe, Americas |
| **Industrial** | Machinery, components | Germany, China |

### Export from Algeria/MENA

| Category | Products | Target Markets |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| **Artisanal Goods** | Ceramics, textiles, leather | Europe, USA, Gulf |
| **Agricultural** | Dates, olive oil, wine | Global |
| **Cultural Products** | Art, antiques, books | Collectors, diaspora |
| **Specialty Materials** | Cork, marble, minerals | Industrial buyers |

## Go-to-Market

### Phase 1: Proof of Concept (Months 1-6)
- Source 10-20 artisan partners in Algeria
- Launch Shopify store with heritage collection
- Test diaspora market (France, Canada, USA)
- Target: $50K revenue

### Phase 2: Expand Product (Months 7-12)
- Add provisions line
- Partner with 2-3 specialty retailers
- Wholesale pilot to restaurants/hotels
- Target: $200K revenue

### Phase 3: Scale Operations (Year 2)
- Establish Algeria logistics hub
- Import line from Europe to Algeria
- B2B partnerships
- Target: $1M revenue
