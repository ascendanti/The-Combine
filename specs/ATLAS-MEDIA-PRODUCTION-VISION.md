## Go-to-Market

### Phase 1: Credential (Months 1-6)
- Complete 1 short documentary (self-funded)
- Pitch to 3-5 festivals
- Build relationships with commissioning editors

### Phase 2: Commission (Months 7-18)
- Secure first broadcast commission
- Complete 3-5 branded content projects
- Build production team core

### Phase 3: Scale (Year 2-3)
- Multiple projects in production
- Archive library generating licensing revenue
- Establish as go-to MENA producer
