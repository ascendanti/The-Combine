## Mission

## Go-to-Market

### Phase 1: Credibility (Months 1-6)
- Publish 12 high-quality free reports
- Guest appearances on podcasts/panels
- Build email list (target: 5,000)

### Phase 2: Monetization (Months 7-12)
- Launch Observer tier
- Convert 2% of list to paid
- Target: 100 subscribers = $50K ARR

### Phase 3: Scale (Year 2)
- Add Analyst tier
- Enterprise pilot with 2-3 clients
- Target: $500K ARR
